Content

Home page

  • Why will people come to your site?

    • People will come to my website because they are personally interested in my life. I have had several time where people have asked me in life what I am doing. But I don't have a good way to share with them, as I am not using social media.

  • What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

    • I expect that people who come to my website will know what is going on in my life. Perhaps they will even have more questions when they leave, then when they came with!

  • What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

    • I hope that people visiting my website will feel uplifted after visiting my blog. I hope to share nice positive posts about my life.

  • What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

    • The only information required will be knowing where to find my blog. I will likely link my blog to a LinkedIn portfolio, and my own personal portfolio. Portfolio

  • Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

    • The only reason why people would come to this site is because they are interested in me. Likely, this will include family and friends.
